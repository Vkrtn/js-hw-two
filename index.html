How to play online?

With a friend. If you want to play with your friend, just press “Get Started” and you will be redirected to the next page. At the top you will see your room’s url which you should send to your friend. The game will begin when another player will enter the room. Both players must open the same url for the game to begin. If the game doesn’t start then you’re in different rooms. Please be sure that you’re on the same url.

Play with a stranger. If you don’t have an opponent to play with you, don’t be upset. You can spend some time playing Rock Paper Scissors with a random stranger. Please click a “Random stranger” button and the game will find an opponent for you. Usually it takes around 10-20 seconds. When the opponent is found, the game will begin automatically.

What are the rules?

The rules are very simple and probably you remember them from your childhood:

  • If you choose Rock, you will win against Scissors but lose against Paper.
  • If you choose Paper, you will win against Rock but lose against Scissors.
  • If you choose Scissors, you will win against Paper but lose against Rock.

There can be 3 to 5 rounds. The game ends when somebody gets 3 wins. If you want to play again, just click “Play again” button after a game is finished.
